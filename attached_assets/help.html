Frequently Asked Questions

View All FAQs
How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

How can I download study materials for offline use?

On any study material page, look for the download icon in the top right corner. Click on it to download the content for offline access. Note that you need to be a premium subscriber to access offline downloads for certain content.

How do I join a scheduled live session?

You can join a live session through the "Live Sessions" section in your dashboard. Click on the scheduled session and then click the "Join Now" button when the session is about to start. Make sure you have a stable internet connection and your audio/video devices are working properly.

Can I get a refund for my subscription?

Yes, we offer a 7-day refund policy for new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 7 days, you can request a full refund through the Billing section in your account settings. For subscriptions older than 7 days, please contact our support team to discuss your situation.

How do I track my study progress?

You can track your study progress through the "Performance Tracking" section in your dashboard. This section provides detailed analytics on completed courses, quiz scores, study hours, and more. You can also set study goals and monitor your progress towards achieving them.
