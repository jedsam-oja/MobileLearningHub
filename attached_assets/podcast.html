Cardiology Concepts: From Basics to Advanced

Dr. Michael Chen 12 Episodes 25K Listeners 4.8 (156 ratings)
Cardiology Medical Education Clinical Practice

Join Dr. Michael Chen, renowned cardiologist and educator, as he takes you through a comprehensive journey into the world of cardiology. From fundamental concepts to advanced clinical applications, this podcast series is designed to enhance your understanding of cardiovascular medicine.

Each episode explores different aspects of cardiology, including anatomy, physiology, pathology, diagnostics, and treatment approaches. Dr. Chen combines evidence-based insights with practical clinical pearls gained from his 20+ years of experience in the field.

Dr. Michael Chen

Dr. Michael Chen is a Professor of Cardiology at Stanford Medical School with over 20 years of clinical and research experience. He is passionate about medical education and making complex concepts accessible to students and practitioners.
